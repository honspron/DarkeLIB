Elementalist

A varient of the Cleric, a Elementalist is a pure elemental spell user that worships a "diety level" elemental intelligence- for each element is an elemental being whose powers are like unto a god. A "supreme elemental" is usually intested in only one thing: the proliferation of his element throughout the planes, and an Elementalist works towards this end. A supreme elemental seeks to dominate places with his element; thus, supreme elementals are in constant conflict.

Type: Pure Spell User
Skills: Limited Combat
Spells: Summoning, attack
Prime Attributes: Int, Wis
Comments: Ohh, God if you only knew!

P.S. Elementalists are a very unique guild among magic users, in that each of their spells represents actually several spells in one. How is this? In addition to training in elementalism, the skill necessary to cast elemental spells, elementalists must also train in one or more elemental lore skills (e.g., fire lore, ice lore, chaos lore) in order to cast the spell using that element. To switch elements, you type 'use ' where the skill is the lore skill you want to use to cast spells. Some elements are more deadly that others, and they cost more to train and are availiable only at higher levels.
