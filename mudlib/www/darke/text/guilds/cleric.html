Cleric

A Cleric's path is highly influenced by his god and many patterns in his life are formed by his religious traning. The difference between a Cleric of a war god and one of a god should be readly apparent. Not only should the two behave differently, reflecting the tenets of their respective religions, but they should have greatly divergent skills.

Skills: Med, Combat
Spells: Healing, Protection, Summoning
Prime Attributes: Wis, Dex
Comments: Fear Pope Fred's Bolt of Righteousness.


NOTE: This guild is understaffed....we need more clerics. Make no mistake, this is one of the more powerful guilds.
