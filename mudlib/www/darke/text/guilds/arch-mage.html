How do I become an Arch-Mage?

1. If you have reincarnated and have an experience bank, it must contain less than 5 levels.
2. You must be familiar with the contents of help newbie, help combat, and help spellsys. You will be asked questions from these files.

If you feel that you meet the requirements above, and want to join, just tell one of the Council members (listed in the table) and we'll let you in...
