Warrior-Monk

The High Warrior Monk is a "fanatic" martial artist who has been brought up in a relatively isolated and restrictive environment dedicated to martial arts and personal disipline. This intensive background leads to relatively low skill development costs in the martial arts and related skills, but relaticely high costs for unrelated skills.

Type: Pure Fighter
Skills: Lots of martial art combat skills
Spells: None
Prime Attributes: Str, Con, Dex
Comments: They rock in hand-to-hand.
