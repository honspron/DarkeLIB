War-Priest

A War Priest's path is highly influenced by his god and many patterns in his life are formed by his religious traning. The difference between a Priest of a war god and one of a god should be readly apparent. Not only should the two behave differently, reflecting the tenets of their respective religions, but they should have greatly divergent skills.

Type: Spell Users
Skills: Med, Combat
Spells: Healing, Protection, Summoning
Prime Attributes: Wis, Dex
Comments: They are more combat oriented than clerics.
