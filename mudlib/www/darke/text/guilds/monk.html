Monk

Monks are Semi spell users. Their base spells deal with personal movement and the control of their own body and the mind while their arms capabilities are concentrated in unarmored, unarmed combat.

Type: Semi-Spell User
Skills: Lots of combat skills
Spells: Body spells like haste etc....
Prime Attributes: Int, Dex
Comments: You are learning grasshopper.
