Paladin

Paladins are semi-spell users who concentrate heavily in chivalric combat. The Paladin is the epitome of Arthurian knighthood. Names like Lancelot, Gawain, and especially Galahad should come to mind. He is a heavy weapons and heavy armour specialist. The Paladin should be played carefully in regard to moral style as any variance from his convictions turns his great strength into bloody hypocrisy.

Type: Semi-Spell User
Skills: Lots of combat skills
Spells: Healing, protection, undead killing
Prime Attributes: Str, Dex, Int
Comments: Those good type knights.
