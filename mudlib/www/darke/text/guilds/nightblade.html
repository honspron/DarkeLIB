Nightblade

The Nightblades are semi-spell users who have trained in skills that are appropiate for covert operations (e.g., assassination, spying, infiltration, etc.) This is class has distinct spells affecting the Nightblades ability to move and distract. There skills specialize in disguises, poisons, and deadly weapons.

Type: Semi-Spell User
Spells: Illusions, movement, poison spells
Prime Attributes: Int, Dex
Comments: They are really kewl!
