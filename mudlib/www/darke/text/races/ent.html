Ent


The Ents arose from early magical experimentation in the area of adding the capacity for thought and movement to plant life. The experiments were firghteningly successful and gave rise to one of the longest-lived and wisest races in the lands of Darkemud. An ent appears exactly as it is: a tall, mobile tree, with four large branches used for manipulation, and four roots used for mobility. Ents' strength and tough bark make them fierce in combat, and their extreme wisdom lends them good spellcasting ability, particularly in the clerical genre.
Note that ents have no true hands or feet, and therefore cannot wear boots or gloves.
