Gromek


Gromeks are a race of homanoid dragons. Their skin is dark green and scaly, and two clawed wings sit above their shoulders. Eons of evolution have robbed them of their breath weapons, but they retain fierce claws and the strength to use them effectively. A few gromeks practice the magical arts, though most prefer combat. A very few are able to become proficient in both.
