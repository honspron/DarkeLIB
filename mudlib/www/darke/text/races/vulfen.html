Vulfen


Furred men with wolfish heads and tails, Vulfen are often confused with werewolves, to the detriment of these shapechangers. Cunning intelligence shapes the behavior of Vulfen, but cruelty and bloodthirst deprive them of human respect and liking. Their own armament of fangs and claws is often supplemented by weapons.
