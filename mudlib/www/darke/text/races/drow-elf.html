Drow-Elf


The Drow elves are an almsot invariably evil race of Elves, whose talents and tendencies lean heavily towards the magical arts. Most are very alert because of the upbringing in a trecherous home. They are cunning and cruel. They tend to dress in flowing garments of black with silver trim. They are ambitious to the end. On rare occasions Drow have been known to repent their evil ways and lean towards a more philanthropic view of life.
