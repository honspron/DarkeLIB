High-Man


High-men are large humans with tough physical stature and good constitution. They are well-suited, it seems, to just about any line of work, and they are touted as some of the quickest learners of skill in the land.

SUMMARY:

SPECIAL: High-men tend to learn skills at a rate higher than most other races. Therefore the development cost of ALL SKILLS AND SPELLS is reduced by 2 for high men.
