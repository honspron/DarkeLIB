Dwarf


Legend and song assert the Dwarves to have been born of stone, naming them as Sons and Daughters of Earth. They are a stalwart people, comfortable below ground, where they delve labyrinthe mines and palatial dwellings. Dwarves are masters of metalcraft. Their works are beautiful as well as durable. Full of ridicule for the ways of conjurers, they employ such magics only to enhance the powers of the weapons and artifacts reluctantly released from their forges. Dwarves are short stocky with ruddy complexions. The men grow long beards which they often braiid. Infertility combined with a lack of females leads few Dwarves to marry or conceicve young. Dwarves favor colorful, hooded clothing and hanf arms. Many are proficient with standard bows, but a likingfor mechanical devices prompts them to employ cross bows when a need for missile weapons arises.
