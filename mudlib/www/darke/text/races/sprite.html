Sprite


Sprites stand about one to two feet with rainbow-tinted gossamer wings. They are extremely agile, but are weak because of their small size. They lead a carefree existance amoung tree tops. Minature cottages and terraces woven from twigs sway with the highest branches in strong windss while the Sprites flit from one to another. Comprising the most fluid and informal assemblage of feys, Sprites occasionally join forces with humanish friends for the novelty of the expereince.
