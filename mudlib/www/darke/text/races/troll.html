Troll


Hideous travesties of sagacious Treeherds, Trolls possess huge, hulking bodies patched by bushes of harsh, wiry hair. Ill-proportioned hands and feet display granite claws, while flattened nostrils deformm a cruel visage. A perpetual snarl distorts the sneering maws of these battle fiends to reveal curving, blood-stained fangs. Grey, stony hides with the rough texture of a cliff face protect the calcified innards or reclusive, solitary Trolls. They also regenerate at incredible speeds and can also regrow lost limbs.
