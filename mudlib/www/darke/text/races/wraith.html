Wraith


Wraiths are one of the few sentient races populating the realms of Darkemud which did not arise from the porgenitors' experiments or the progenitors themselves. They are a quasi-undead race, arising from an undead spirit inhabiting a living body. Their souls become merged, and the recipient body is drawn partially into the ethereal plane, allowing only partial contact with the prime material world. The touch of the wraith is chilling, and this is often used as a deadly weapon. Wraiths have been assimilated well into society, and find themselves in a variety of roles.
