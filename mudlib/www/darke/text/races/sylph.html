Sylph


Sylphs are beautiful, humanoid women with wings like dragonflies. Their wings are 4-5 feet long and translucent, clear or spotted with iridescent color. Their long bright hair may be and 'normal' color, or blue, purple, or green. They wear flowing diaphanous robes which accent their wings or hair. Sylphs are related to air elementals and to nymphs, perhaps originating as a cross between nymphs and aerial servants. Sylphs can speak common and their own musical language.
