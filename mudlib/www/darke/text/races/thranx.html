Thranx


Thranx are large four armed insectiods. They are encased in the armour of a chitinous exoskeleton. They stand erect on their third, strengthened pair of legs. The middle set and first set can be used to manipulate objects. Large, complex eeyes at the front of the head process visual information. Razor-edged mandibles outside of the mouth shred food before it is introduced to the inner chelicerae. Thranx communicate primarily through touch and taste, generating complex biochemicals that process semantic meaning for those of their race. They can also learn to produce the aural sounds that comprise of the languages of most other species.
