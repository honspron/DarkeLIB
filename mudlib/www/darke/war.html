DarkeMUD Warz


The DarkeMUD war will be a mudwide war where everyone fights. You will suffer no penalties of death. It is just for fun and pride etc... See who is the kewlest. This will happen twice a day when reboots come. I have seen war systems on many other muds and I actually like them... so we have to have one. I will outline a little of what we have planeed for the war system.
  1. It will happen twice a day as I said after reboots.
  2. There will be individual wars, team wars, and guild wars.
  3. There will be some kind of prize. (That is up in the air)
  4. All shops will be closed, castles will be locked off, you will not be able to use your pets.
  5. There will be a 15-20 min prep time where you can cast spells, kill stuff on the mud for equip, and spend war-money at the war shop.
  6. There will be various items you can buy there and armours & weapons.
  7. A) Ind wars.... everyone for themselves!
    B) Guild wars... the guild left standing wins. Every member in the winning guild who is still alive will get something.
    C) Team wars... Team wars will be divided with the top tanks and mages divided evenly to the teams. You can form parties in team wars! The living memebers in the winning team gets some prize.

This is just a rough outline... It will be more specific when we get ready to code it.

++Drizzt 4/7/96
